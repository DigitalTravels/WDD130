Overview

Purpose

For a while now, I have been wanting to build a site for me and my friends to post stuff about games/life. It would also be used to schedual large scale group-meetups. In other words, it would be like a personal social media site.

Audience

The audiance would be anyone looking to play games with alright people. I would also want to use it to plan camping trips, parties, etc.
